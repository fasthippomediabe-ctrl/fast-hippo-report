Exact entries to add — copy-paste ready — modeled after #2-ranked AAA Digital Marketing Agency

How to Add These in Google Business Profile

GBP Dashboard → Edit products → + Add product

For each product below, you'll fill in: Product name, Category (creates a grouping header), Description (max 1,000 chars), Price (optional), and Button (link to your service page).

Upload a branded image (minimum 720×720px, JPG or PNG) for each product. Use real photos or branded graphics — not stock photos.
