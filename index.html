  • Multi-location SEO — Seota targets this; with three offices you have credibility to compete here

  • Osky Blue's SMB loyalty — 500+ websites and BBB A+ rating create strong trust with Frisco small businesses

  • Competitor adoption of AI — as AI marketing becomes mainstream, current differentiators may narrow
